Return Policy

Products can be returned within 7 days of delivery.

Items must be unused, undamaged, and in their original packaging with invoice included.

Returns will not be accepted for products that are physically damaged by customers after delivery.

Products purchased during special sale events are eligible only for replacement and not refund.

Customers must initiate return requests through customer support or the official website.


Refund Policy

Refunds are processed after successful inspection of returned products.

Refund processing usually takes 5–7 business days.

Refunds will be credited to the original payment method used during purchase.

For cash on delivery orders, refunds are transferred through bank account or UPI.

Shipping charges are non-refundable unless the product is defective or incorrect.


Damaged or Defective Products

Customers receiving damaged, broken, or defective products are eligible for replacement or full refund.

Customers should upload product images while raising complaints.

Replacement requests must be raised within 48 hours of delivery.

Our support team may request additional proof before approving refunds.


Shipping Policy

Standard shipping takes 3–7 business days depending on location.

Express delivery usually takes 1–2 business days.

International shipping may require 7–15 business days.

Shipping delays may occur during festivals, holidays, or unexpected weather conditions.


Cancellation Policy

Orders can only be cancelled before shipment.

Once shipped, cancellation is not possible, but customers may initiate a return request after delivery.

Refunds for cancelled prepaid orders are processed within 3 business days.


Exchange Policy

Customers can request size or color exchange within 5 days of delivery.

Exchange requests depend on stock availability.

Used or damaged products are not eligible for exchange.


Warranty Policy

Electronic products include a 1-year manufacturer warranty.

Warranty does not cover accidental damage, water damage, or unauthorized repairs.

Customers should retain original invoice for warranty claims.
